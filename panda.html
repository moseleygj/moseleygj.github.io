Project Panda

The Goal

A web-based music player with the ability to play and download individual songs or entire albums. This project seemed simple at first, but after creating it, I have a new respect for developers who make music players. The SQL and data ingestion took the most amount of time. This was fully functional and completed in about two months while working on it off and on. Although it works, I'm contemplating whether to dive into the rabbit hole of playlists and user accounts...
